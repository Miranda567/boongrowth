WE ARE Boongrowth Capital

In the insurance sector, managing assets is essential. At Boongrowth Capital, we invest customer premiums to fulfill long-term responsibilities. Over 750 billion dollars' worth of assets, spread across a variety of asset classes, industries, and nations, make up our investment portfolio.

Private equity, infrastructure, and renewable energy investments are the main areas of our attention.

As a result, Boongrowth Capital is interconnected both directly and indirectly with numerous companies, industries, and economies, making it an essential part of the global economy.


We aim to transform the traditional wealth management industry by combining decades of academic research on financial markets with the latest technologies and machine learning advancements.
